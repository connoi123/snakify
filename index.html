About Snake


The Philippines is a popular holiday destination for its
beautiful coastline, clear waters, and stunning greenery,
not to mention its fantastic weather.

However, these factors are also why its 7,641 islands
are home to a wide variety of wildlife, including snakes.

The Philippines is home to a variety of different snake
species and some of them are indeed venomous, including
a range of cobras, vipers, coral snakes and sea snakes
which can potentially kill a human if they feel threatened.
